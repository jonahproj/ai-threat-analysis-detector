# Week 4: Model Comparison

Tested 4 AI models on 5 Cybersecurity text samples to evaluate their suitability for the Relevance Scorer component of our Threat Intelligence Feed Dashboard.

## Models Tested 

- HF Sentiment (distilbert-sst-2) 
- HF Zero-Shot (bart-large-mnli) 
- HF NER (bert-large-NER)
- Groq Llama 3 8B

## Finding 

Recommended Groq Llama 3 8B for the Relevance Scorer because of its speed, efficiency, and scalability.

See `report.md` for full analysis.
